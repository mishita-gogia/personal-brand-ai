# PersonalBrand AI

## Overview

PersonalBrand AI is an AI-powered LinkedIn content and personal branding assistant built using Google AI Studio and Gemini.

The project helps users create engaging LinkedIn content while improving their professional online presence through AI-generated recommendations and personalized branding strategies.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students and professionals struggle to:

* Write engaging LinkedIn posts
* Build a strong personal brand
* Maintain consistency in content creation
* Optimize their LinkedIn profiles

PersonalBrand AI addresses these challenges by generating professional content and branding suggestions instantly.

---

## Features

### LinkedIn Post Generation

Creates professional and engaging LinkedIn posts based on user achievements and experiences.

### Multiple Content Styles

Supports:

* Professional
* Inspirational
* Storytelling

### Hashtag Suggestions

Generates relevant hashtags to increase post visibility and engagement.

### Headline Recommendations

Provides optimized LinkedIn headline suggestions.

### Personal Brand Analysis

Identifies strengths, keywords, and content pillars for professional growth.

### Content Calendar

Generates future content ideas to maintain consistency.

### Profile Optimization

Offers suggestions for improving profile sections and overall branding.

---

## Technologies Used

* Google AI Studio

---

## Workflow

1. User enters professional details.
2. AI analyzes the input.
3. AI generates LinkedIn content.
4. AI suggests branding improvements.
5. User receives a complete personal branding package.
